Iskandar Puteri — School Guide

Sunway International School Iskandar Puteri: Is It Worth Living Nearby?

Sam Tee Property Advisor · REN 80322
July 2026

For families relocating to Iskandar Puteri, the school decision is often the most important factor in the property search. When families commit to Sunway International School (SIS) — one of the few schools in JB offering both a Canadian curriculum and the International Baccalaureate — the next question is always: where should we buy? This guide answers that question honestly.

What Sunway International School offers

Sunway International School Iskandar Puteri is located within the Sunway City Iskandar Puteri township. It offers a dual-track curriculum:

  • Canadian (Ontario) curriculum — from Kindergarten through Grade 12, following the Ontario Ministry of Education framework. The Ontario curriculum is internationally recognised and provides a clear pathway to Canadian universities as well as universities in other English-speaking countries.
  • International Baccalaureate (IB) — SIS offers IB programming integrated with the Ontario curriculum. The IB Diploma Programme (for Grades 11–12) is one of the most widely accepted pre-university qualifications globally, accepted by universities in the UK, US, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and most European countries.

The combination of Ontario curriculum with IB availability from the same school is relatively rare in the JB market. Most competing international schools in Iskandar Puteri offer either a British curriculum (IGCSE/A-Level) or a standalone IB programme — not the Canadian/IB hybrid that SIS provides.

School hours: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

Who the Canadian/IB curriculum suits

The Ontario/IB curriculum at SIS is particularly well-suited to:

  • Families with Canadian citizenship or PR who want their children to maintain familiarity with the Canadian curriculum for eventual return or post-secondary education in Canada
  • Families who want the IB Diploma as a university entrance qualification — particularly those targeting universities in the UK, Europe, or seeking a globally recognised qualification
  • Families who value a North American educational philosophy (emphasis on critical thinking, project-based learning, co-curricular development) over a more exam-focused British model
  • Families coming from international schooling backgrounds in Canada, the US, or countries using the IB system

Where to live if your children attend SIS

Option 1: Within Sunway City (Signature Home and other precincts)

Living within Sunway City Iskandar Puteri is the most convenient option for SIS families. Students can walk or take a short internal shuttle to school — no school bus logistics, no daily car commute, no traffic uncertainty. Parents can drop children at school and walk back home in minutes.

The trade-off is primarily tenure: most Sunway City residential products are leasehold. For families who are prioritising school convenience over freehold land title, this may be an acceptable trade. For families who want freehold property, they will need to assess whether the school convenience justifies accepting leasehold tenure.

Option 2: Nearby communities within 10–15 minutes

Families who want freehold landed property but are committed to SIS often consider communities within a 10–15 minute drive of Sunway City. These include parts of Bukit Indah, the Nusajaya fringe, and some landed estates in the western Iskandar Puteri corridor. The trade-off is a daily school run by car — manageable, but requiring reliable transport and daily time commitment.

School bus services from SIS cover certain residential catchment areas — verify the current bus routes with the school before buying based on an assumed bus service.

Option 3: Communities near other international schools with planned transfer to SIS

Some families start in communities near other international schools (e.g., Horizon Hills, where EtonHouse and other schools are accessible) and plan to transfer to SIS when their children reach a certain age. This is feasible but introduces transition risk — curriculum changes mid-schooling can be disruptive, and the IB structure at SIS has specific entry points that are easier to enter at younger ages.

The property premium for SIS proximity

Proximity to a strong international school creates a measurable property premium in most established markets — and JB is no exception. Properties within the Sunway City boundary, and those within a 5-minute drive of SIS, carry a premium relative to otherwise comparable properties further away. This premium has both a positive and negative implication: if you buy near SIS, you benefit from the premium when you sell; but you also pay it when you buy.

For families who plan to be in JB for 5–10+ years and whose school choice is fixed, paying the SIS proximity premium is a reasonable decision — the lifestyle value over a 10-year school horizon is real and the premium is supported by consistent demand from the family buyer segment.

SIS vs other international schools in Iskandar Puteri

The main international school alternatives in Iskandar Puteri include EtonHouse International School (British curriculum), Raffles American School (US curriculum), and Marlborough College Malaysia (British curriculum). Each serves a different curriculum and buyer profile:

  • EtonHouse — British curriculum, strong early years programme, located in the Nusajaya/Horizon Hills area
  • Raffles American School — US curriculum (AP programme), popular with American and international families seeking a US-style education
  • Marlborough College — Premium British boarding school model, very high fees, targets a specific demographic

SIS's Canadian/IB positioning is distinct from all three — it is the only school in Iskandar Puteri offering this specific curriculum combination on this scale.

Sam's take

If your family is committed to the Canadian/IB curriculum and SIS is the school, the property decision for where to live becomes much simpler: either within Sunway City for maximum convenience, or within a 10–15 minute drive for freehold landed options. The school choice should drive the property choice, not the other way around. Get in touch if you want a shortlist of specific communities that work well for SIS families in your budget range.
