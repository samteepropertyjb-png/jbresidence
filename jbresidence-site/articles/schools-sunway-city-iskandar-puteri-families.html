Iskandar Puteri — Family Guide

Schools in Sunway City Iskandar Puteri: Full Guide for Families 2026

Sam Tee Property Advisor · REN 80322
July 2026

One of the most frequent questions I get from families considering Iskandar Puteri is: which community has the best schools within or close to the township? For families who want international-standard education within the same development they live in, Sunway City Iskandar Puteri has one of the most comprehensive education ecosystems in the district. This guide covers every institution inside the township.

Why school proximity matters for property choice

For families with school-age children, the school decision often drives the property decision — not the other way around. A family committed to a specific curriculum (IB, Canadian, British, or Chinese) will typically buy where that school is accessible without a long daily commute for their children. In Iskandar Puteri, the township with the best on-site school ecosystem has a structural advantage for the family buyer segment — and that is Sunway City.

Sunway International School

Sunway International School (SIS) is the anchor educational institution within Sunway City Iskandar Puteri. It offers a blend of the Canadian Ontario curriculum and the International Baccalaureate (IB) programme, running from Kindergarten through Grade 12. This is a relatively rare combination — most international schools in the JB market offer either a British or an IB-only curriculum. The Canadian/IB hybrid positions SIS well for families who want strong academic rigour with a pathway to IB Diploma for university entry.

The school is physically located within the Sunway City township, meaning students living in Sunway City residential precincts can walk or take a short internal shuttle to school — a genuine daily lifestyle advantage that no other community in Iskandar Puteri offers at this scale.

Hours: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

SJK(C) Cheah Fah

SJK(C) Cheah Fah is a national Chinese primary school (SRJK) located within the Sunway City township. What makes it unusual is the quality of its physical infrastructure — described as having international school-level facilities, it is also the first school in Malaysia to receive a GreenRE Platinum certification, the highest tier of green building recognition in Malaysia.

For Chinese-Malaysian families who want their children in the national Chinese school system but with modern, well-maintained facilities in a safe environment, Cheah Fah is a significant draw. The presence of a GreenRE Platinum school within a gated township community is a meaningful differentiator from competing communities where the nearest Chinese primary school requires a car journey.

Hours: 7:30 AM – 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday and Sunday.

The Treehouse Playschool & Kindergarten

The Treehouse Playschool is the early childhood option within Sunway City, offering a nature-integrated, play-based learning approach inspired by the Reggio Emilia philosophy. It caters to the preschool and kindergarten age group, providing a natural bridge to either Sunway International School or SJK(C) Cheah Fah for primary school years.

For families moving to Sunway City with very young children, the ability to start their education within the same township from the earliest years — without requiring school bus arrangements or daily car drives to a separate location — is a practical and meaningful advantage.

Hours: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

Enrichment and skills schools

Beyond the academic schools, Sunway City hosts a dense cluster of enrichment, sports, and skills providers that most comparable communities cannot match:

  • 42 Iskandar Puteri — A free coding school using peer-to-peer learning methodology (the 42 Network), open to students aged 18 and above. Unusual for a township to host this calibre of tech education.
  • Happy Fish Swim School — Swim lessons for all ages, open daily from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Year-round swimming in a dedicated school environment is a significant lifestyle asset.
  • Horse XPark — Equestrian lessons for children from age 7, operating within the XPark facility. Equestrian access within a township is rare in any Malaysian residential development.
  • Artemis Gymnastics — Gymnastics training for children from age 4.
  • Joyous Skill School — Life skills education for ages 7–12.
  • PY Academy & Easy Education Centre — English tuition and academic support for primary-age children.
  • The Leaders Academy — ESL and robotics courses.
  • Luna & Solar Academy — English immersion and summer camps.

Higher education: Sunway TES

For older students or working adults, Sunway TES within the township offers professional accounting qualifications — ACCA, CFAB, MICPA, CPA Australia, and CFA. This is not a typical secondary school or university programme, but it does mean the township has a genuine further-education option on-site.

What this means for property buyers

No other single community in Iskandar Puteri — not Horizon Hills, not Eco Botanic, not East Ledang — has this density of educational options within the same township boundary. For a family where both international school enrolment and Chinese primary school access matter (common among Malaysian families who want bilingual education pathways), Sunway City is the only development that offers both under one roof.

The practical implication for property buyers: families who commit to Sunway International School or SJK(C) Cheah Fah for their children are very likely to also buy within Sunway City. The school ecosystem is the single strongest retention factor for the Sunway City buyer community — and that resident stability has a positive effect on the property market within the township.

Sam's take

I work with a lot of families for whom the school question is the starting point of the property conversation, not an afterthought. If Sunway International School's curriculum is the right fit for your children, buying within Sunway City is a logical extension of that decision. If you are looking at Chinese primary school options, Cheah Fah's GreenRE Platinum facilities within a safe, well-managed township community are genuinely hard to match elsewhere.
