Who is Sam Tee JB Property?

Sam Tee is a licensed property advisor (REN 80322) based in Johor Bahru, Malaysia, specialising in residential property across Iskandar Puteri, Forest City, and JB Town. Sam runs The JB Residence — the independent property knowledge base for Johor Bahru — and the YouTube channel Sam Tee JB Property, where he has published over 92 videos covering the JB property market honestly and without developer bias.

His approach is straightforward: give buyers the information they need to make a good decision, whether or not that decision results in a transaction with him. This includes telling buyers when a property is not right for them, which area suits their actual lifestyle, and what the risks are that most agents do not mention.

Sam works with Malaysian families, Singaporean buyers, foreign nationals on MM2H, retirees planning a lifestyle move, and investors building long-term property portfolios in Johor.
